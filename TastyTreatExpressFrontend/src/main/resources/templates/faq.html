FAQ's

1. Who is a Tasty Treat Express Delivery Partner? +
Tasty Treat Express Delivery Partners are independent contractors, who are required to pick up food or grocery items ordered by customers from restaurants / outlets and deliver to customers. Delivery Partners use their own vehicles (Bike/Cycle) to deliver orders to Tasty Treat Express customers.
2. How to become a Tasty Treat Express Delivery Partner? +
To become a Tasty Treat Express Delivery Partner, visit the Tasty Treat Express website or app, navigate to the "Become a Delivery Partner" section, and follow the registration process. You will need to provide identification, vehicle details, and other required documents.
3. What are the requirements to become a Tasty Treat Express Delivery Partner? +
Requirements include a valid ID proof, a two-wheeler or bicycle, a smartphone, and the ability to work flexible hours. A background check may also be conducted.
4. What are the login hours? +
Login hours are flexible, allowing partners to choose their working hours based on availability, typically between 8 AM and 11 PM.
5. When do I start delivering orders? +
You can start delivering orders after completing the registration process and receiving approval from Tasty Treat Express, which may take a few days.
6. How much can I earn? +
Earnings vary based on the number of deliveries, distance, and incentives, with potential to earn between $2-$5 per delivery on average.
7. How will I get paid? +
Payments are made weekly via bank transfer or a partnered payment app, depending on your location and preferences.
8. Are there any bonuses? +
Yes, bonuses are available based on performance, such as completing a certain number of deliveries or working during peak hours.
9. Do we have to pay any fees to get on board as a Delivery Partner? +
No, there are no fees to join as a Delivery Partner; Tasty Treat Express does not charge for registration.
10. What are the benefits of joining as a Delivery Partner other than earnings? +
Benefits include flexible working hours, access to training, and potential career growth opportunities within Tasty Treat Express.
11. How do I delete my account? +
To delete your account, contact Tasty Treat Express support through the app or website and submit a request, which will be processed within a few days.
