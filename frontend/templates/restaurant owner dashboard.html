Frequently Asked Questions
How do I update my outlet's operating hours?
You can update your operating hours in the "Outlet Info" section. Click on "Edit Details" and modify the hours as needed.
What should I do if a customer rejects an order?
If a customer rejects an order, you can view the details in the "Order History" section. Contact the customer to understand the reason and resolve the issue. You can also reach out to support for assistance.
How can I create a new offer?
Go to the "Offers" section and click on "Create Offer". Fill in the details such as offer title, discount value, and validity period, then save the offer.
